The Situation

You Did Not Plan
for This.

A single accusation in Colombia can freeze assets, confiscate a passport, and trigger preventive detention — before any trial. The legal process runs in Spanish. The judiciary operates by rules that are opaque to outsiders.

The first 72 hours are decisive. Most foreign nationals make irreversible mistakes in this window — not because they are guilty, but because they do not know the terrain.

ShieldLegal was built for exactly this moment: to stand between you and a system you did not choose.

"The system doesn't wait for you to understand it. You need someone who already does."

Passport Confiscation

Colombian prosecutors can request travel bans on foreign nationals within hours of a formal complaint being filed.

Preventive Detention

Without proper representation at the imputación hearing, pretrial detention becomes significantly more likely.

Language Barrier

Misstatements during initial police contact — even innocent ones — become part of the permanent legal record.

Families Left Without Information

Relatives abroad are often the last to know and the first to panic. We communicate directly with your family in English.

Reputational Damage

Without early intervention, narrative control becomes nearly impossible as Colombian news cycles accelerate.
